HOTEL ROYAL has been mastering the fine art of Hospitality and Care for nearly three decades. We understand our guests' needs, tastes and preferences quite well, providing prompt and personalized services round the clock
Conferences Seminars Halls
To reach out to your business associates, be it large or small through Conferences, Seminars, Parties or Get-to-gathers, you have a choice of banquet halls to suite your requirement.
Restaurant
HOTEL ROYAL serves a rich array of mouth watering cuisine from North to South and East to West valuing beauty and grace in every aspect of life. “Nano”, the vegetarian and Non- vegetarian multi cuisine restaurant at Royal serves gastronomic delights ranging from Indian, Chinese and Continental cuisines to regional flavors. While lunch (11am - 3pm) and dinner (7pm - 11pm) are served at fixed timings Snacks and beverages are available round and clock through room service. Guests can also enjoy their meals in the comfort of their rooms.
Outdoor Catering
HOTEL ROYAL also offers outdoor catering services in and around the city of Ahmedabad at a menu of your choice from 50 to 5000 persons to take care of your events such as marriages, conferences, social gatherings and other events with a range of choice for menus, exclusive decor of flower and faraskhana.
Banquets
We have 2 A/C Banquet Halls, 1 multipurpose Air cooled to accommodate from up to 400 persons with range of choice for Menus to entertain all your functions from Conferences to Marriages & Social Gatherings to Business Seminars.
