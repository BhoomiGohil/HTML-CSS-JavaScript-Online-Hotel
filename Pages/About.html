About Hotel
Welcome to Hotel Royal. At Royal, we believe a star hotel should offer more than a good night's sleep and comfort. It should create an environment that awakens your senses. It's one of the many intangible qualities you'll experience at our hotel.

Ahmedabad is the commercial capital of the state of Gujarat. Hotel is situated in a prime business location at the junction of Ahmedabad – North –West Gujarat (Saurastra) and end of Sarkhej-Gandhinagar, 20 km from Airport, 10 km from railway station and 4 km from bus stand.
